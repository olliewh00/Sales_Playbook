Sales Playbook Notes

1. Lead Stage
- Goal: qualify inbound and outbound leads quickly.
- Core KPIs: response time, lead-to-discovery conversion.
- Action: run a short qualification script (budget, authority, need, timeline).

2. Discovery Stage
- Goal: uncover measurable business pain and desired outcomes.
- Core KPIs: discovery-to-proposal conversion, meeting quality score.
- Action: ask at least 5 problem-depth questions and summarize pain in customer language.

3. Proposal Stage
- Goal: map product capabilities to quantified business value.
- Core KPIs: proposal acceptance rate, average time in stage.
- Action: include ROI estimate, implementation plan, and risk mitigation.

4. Closing Stage
- Goal: remove final blockers and confirm decision process.
- Core KPIs: win rate, cycle length.
- Action: identify legal/procurement constraints early and define clear next-step commitments.

Objection Handling
- Price objection: re-anchor to ROI and total cost of delay.
- Timing objection: co-create phased rollout with low-risk pilot scope.
- Competitor objection: compare outcomes, not features.
